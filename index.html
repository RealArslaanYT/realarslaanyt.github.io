Hey,

My name is Arslaan Pathan.


I’m 12 years old and I like computer science, writing stories (sometimes), Linux, FOSS (Free Open-Source Software), hacking, and reverse-engineering. One of my best (and most recent) projects is a (currently unfinished) fighter game titled Showdown of the Sticks, inspired by Alan Becker’s upcoming game Animation VERSUS.


I mainly use Fedora Linux on my M2 MacBook Pro (thanks to the Asahi Linux project) for daily-driving and most development/coding, and use MacOS for gaming or specific development such as Unity, SDL2, and Arduino. My favorite games are Minecraft and Stick Fight: The Game. I’ve been programming and tinkering with devices since the age of six. What originally got me into programming was looking at my old iPad 2 (which I still have as of 2025, six years later) and saying – “how are these apps made?”


Feel free to explore my projects, stories, and achievements, and thanks for stopping by!
